Example Engagement

Context

A global Tier-1 automotive supplier preparing for the launch of a new high-complexity product faced instability across engineering, supplier readiness, and production processes.

Challenge

  • Late-stage design changes impacting manufacturing readiness
  • Quality deviations across multiple sites
  • Limited alignment between R&D, operations, and suppliers
  • Escalating risk of delayed SOP

VAIRA Contribution

  • Rapid diagnostic of technical and organizational root causes
  • Structured cross-functional alignment
  • Stabilization of supplier interfaces
  • Definition of industrialization roadmap
  • Coaching of program leadership

Impact

  • 35% defect reduction within 4 months
  • Stabilized launch timeline
  • Improved transparency and governance
  • Sustainable operational model
