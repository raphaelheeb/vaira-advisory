Case Detail: Tier-1 Automotive Supplier

Context

A global Tier-1 automotive supplier preparing for the launch of a high-complexity product faced instability across engineering, supplier readiness, and production processes.

Challenge

  • Late-stage design changes impacting manufacturing readiness
  • Quality deviations across multiple production sites
  • Limited alignment between R&D, operations, and suppliers
  • Escalating risk of delayed SOP and cost overruns

VAIRA Contribution

  • Rapid diagnostic of technical and organizational root causes
  • Cross-functional alignment across engineering, quality, and operations
  • Stabilization of supplier escalation mechanisms
  • Definition of structured industrialization roadmap
  • Coaching of senior leadership and program teams

Impact

  • 35% defect reduction within 4 months
  • Stabilized launch timeline and avoided delay costs
  • Improved transparency and decision-making across sites
  • Sustainable governance model implemented

Key Themes: Industrialization · Cross-functional alignment · Supplier management · Quality stabilization
