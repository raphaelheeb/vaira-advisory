Digital Manufacturing Architecture for a High‑Precision Components Producer

Industry 4.0 · MES Integration · Data Architecture

A precision components manufacturer needed a unified digital manufacturing architecture to replace fragmented shop‑floor data flows and inconsistent reporting.

  • Modular data architecture for shop‑floor systems
  • MES–ERP integration patterns
  • Unified telemetry model for CNC and inspection systems
  • Data governance for quality and traceability

Impact: Faster root‑cause analysis and reduced manual reporting effort.

Predictive Maintenance Framework for Industrial Equipment Fleet

AI/ML · Sensor Fusion · Reliability Engineering

An equipment manufacturer aimed to transition from reactive service operations to predictive maintenance.

  • Sensor fusion across vibration, thermal, and operational data
  • Scalable analytics pipeline for anomaly detection
  • Feature engineering for mechanical degradation
  • Reliability‑centered maintenance model

Impact: Early detection of failure signatures and reduced unplanned downtime.

End‑to‑End Process Optimization for a High‑Mix, Low‑Volume Factory

Operations Engineering · Digital Twins · Flow Optimization

A high‑mix electronics manufacturer struggled with long changeovers and variable takt.

  • Digital twin of production flow
  • Queueing and throughput analysis
  • New scheduling logic for high‑mix production
  • Data‑driven changeover strategy

Impact: Higher throughput and more predictable delivery performance.
